Who am I?

I wonder who.

I'm Potato22, my name has quite a "few" aliases. They were:

  • Potato2292
  • Pot
  • Mary
  • Oulie
  • I.. don't have to list all of them you get the idea.

I was born on 22nd September, I live in Indonesia. Most of you probably came here either from my art page Deviant Art or my Twitter or maybe somewhere else. And you'd probably know what I often do, I make vectors of ponies. I never really sure when people ask why do I like ponies so much, so I just reply them back with why the heck not? I started vectoring from February 3rd, 2018, It's been a ride, lots-lots of hills and mountains, offroad included.

There's a period of time where I haven't gotten into vectoring, I was a Photoshop editor back then. I make PIRLS (Ponies In Real Life) pictures, I pick a pony vector from someone else and then pick a real-life background and then, composite the two, Viola. A PIRL creation. 

Then I moved to a little bit of drawing with an online art tool, which only lasts a few weeks before I gave up on it. Then I met Arifproject a vector artist who creates unique vectors out of an image website platform so-called Derpibooru

He's the first person who ever gave a big spark to do vectoring, and I got on to it! :D After.. going through several give-ups. I managed to do the first ever vectoring work of my early design pony character.

There she is, she's alive since then. Meet Oulie Mareota, this is her early design, she had some changes now.

I'm so proud of her.
